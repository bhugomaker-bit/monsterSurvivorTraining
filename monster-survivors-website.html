About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an exciting survival game where you must battle against endless waves of monsters. As you progress, you'll unlock new abilities, weapons, and power-ups to help you survive longer.

The game features a unique roguelike experience where each playthrough is different. Collect experience points to level up and choose from a variety of upgrades to customize your character's abilities.

Controls

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character. Attacks are automatic when enemies are in range.

Game Strategies

Early Game

Focus on movement speed and area damage abilities early on. Stay mobile and try to group enemies together to maximize your damage output.

Mid Game

Balance your upgrades between offensive and defensive abilities. Look for synergies between different weapons and passive items.

Late Game

Prioritize survival over aggression. Use the entire map to your advantage and don't get cornered by large groups of monsters.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Monster Survivors free to play?

Yes, Monster Survivors is completely free to play online through our website.

Can I play on mobile devices?

Yes, Monster Survivors is optimized for both desktop and mobile play. The controls will automatically adapt to your device.

How do I unlock new characters?

New characters can be unlocked by achieving specific milestones in the game, such as surviving for a certain amount of time or defeating special boss monsters.

Does the game save my progress?

The game saves your unlocks and achievements in your browser's local storage. For the best experience, play on the same device and browser.
